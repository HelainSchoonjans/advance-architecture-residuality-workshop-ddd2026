## Confortable with uncertainty

A senior architect can work with people that don't know what they want.

Architecture is about coping with changes.

Alan Turing: we can never know ahead of time if a program will work.

## History of paradigms

1936: Logical phase. Software can be reduced to logic.
1968: Structural phase: structures. Value streams, process and org charts. Enterprise architecture.
1979: Adaptive phase: bizz env is changing. Complex and impredictable. Meets complexity through process. Agile processes.
2015: Residual phase: Software is ordered, reality is complex.
